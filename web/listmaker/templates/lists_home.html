What are lists?

Custom lists allow you to create 'lists' or groupings of recipients on this site. You can create ad-hoc lists and optionally save and publish your lists for others to see.

You could think of custom lists like a mix between a shipping cart and an amazon wish list: you add items to it like you might on any ecommerce web site, and then save the list for others to see at a later date.
